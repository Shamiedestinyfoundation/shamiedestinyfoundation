About Us

Empowering Children. Transforming Communities.

Shamie Destiny Foundation is a community-centered, non-profit organization dedicated to creating a safer society where every child can grow, thrive, and reach their full potential. We believe that when children are protected, supported, and empowered, communities become stronger and more hopeful. Guided by our motto, “A safer society for a safer child,” we work to uplift children and youth facing difficult circumstances by offering hope, care, and opportunities for a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can learn, play, and grow confidently. We are committed to supporting each child’s physical, emotional, social, and intellectual development through quality care, structured learning, and creative play.

  • Safe & secure learning environment
  • Child-centered teaching approach
  • Holistic child development

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted early childhood education center that inspires lifelong learning, creativity, and confidence in every child. We aim to shape responsible, curious, and compassionate individuals prepared for future academic and life success.

  • Excellence in early education
  • Strong moral and social values
  • Preparation for future learning
